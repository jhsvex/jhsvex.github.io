About

Student run program focused on engineering excellence, mentorship, and competition results.

What we do

Plan, prototype, and test robots for the VEX V5 competition game. Members learn design, fabrication, control theory, and software. Veterans mentor rookies to grow capability across the club.

  • mechanical design
  • embedded programming
  • sensors and control
  • strategy and scouting
  • outreach
Workspace

Build Lab

Benchtop layout with stock control, shared tools, and inspection checks before practice runs.

Practice

Field

Regulation field for driver practice and autonomous testing with routine maintenance logs.

Mentorship

Rookie Pods

Small groups led by veterans to speed up onboarding for CAD, programming, and match prep.
